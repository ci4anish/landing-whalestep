Hirescapes
Even though we live in the 21st century, service providers and customers are still looking for each other through acquaintances or specialized exhibitions, especially in the Hardscaping field. The main requirement was to create a platform where they can do this without leaving home and concentrate on the more important stuff in life. In brief, we divided UX flow into three parts - users post projects, contractors review them and give estimates, admin moderates the process. The end goal of these actions is a Contract with payments through milestones. Monetization is a percentage of all money that went through the application. Easy to say hard to do, but we’re not afraid of work😎.

The application is fully responsive(supports various screens), filled with features designed to replace the current details of contracting offline(sophisticated estimation and moderation functionality, chat, online payments, etc.) and introduces new ones that make online contracting better(as Pinterest like “Idea board” where you can dive into fresh ideas of how to make your yard perfect).

The result didn’t have to wait. During the first three months of work, more than one hundred contracts were executed through the platform. We are proud to be involved in the success of our client and wish him all the best and greater achievements.💪
